## About Prime95 Stress Test
**Prime95 Stress Test** is a highly respected utility originally developed for prime number calculations, now widely adopted as the industry benchmark for CPU and memory stability testing.  
The **Prime95 Stress Test** runs complex FFT-based computations that generate extreme heat and power draw, exposing any weakness in cooling, voltage regulation, or core stability.  
Unlike basic diagnostic tools, the **Prime95 Stress Test** pushes every instruction set (AVX, AVX2, AVX-512) to its limit, ensuring that your system can handle sustained 100% load without errors.  
For professionals building workstations or gaming rigs, the **Prime95 Stress Test** is the final verification step before deployment.  
The **Prime95 Stress Test** also detects RAM corruption, motherboard power phase issues, and insufficient thermal solutions.  
Regular use of **Prime95 Stress Test** helps prevent silent data corruption caused by unstable overclocks.  
Every serious hardware reviewer relies on **Prime95 Stress Test** for reproducible performance metrics.
